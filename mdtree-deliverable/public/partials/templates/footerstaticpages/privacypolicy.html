What Are Cookies and How Does the MDtree Service Use Them?

We may use cookies to collect or log certain information. A cookie is a small piece of information that the MDtree Service stores on a personal computing devices and which it can later retrieve. We may use cookies for some administrative purposes, for example, to store our customers' preferences for certain kinds of services or to store a password so that you do not have to input it every time you visit our MDtree Service. The cookies will not contain personal information that will enable anyone to contact you via email or other means. If you are uncomfortable with the use of cookie technology, you can set your browser to refuse cookies. Certain services, however, could be dependent on cookies and you may disable those services by refusing cookies.
